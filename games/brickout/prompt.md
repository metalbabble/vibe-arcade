Generate a new project for a game similar to the classic game "breakout". In this game players will use the arrow keys on the keyboard to move a paddle left and right along the bottom of the screen. The objective is to bounce a ball against bricks that appear across the top of the screen.

- Draw a grid of randomly colored "bricks" across the top third of the screen. Leave a little room at the top of the screen.
- A ball starts in the middle of the screen and starts traveling at a random angle towards the bottom of the screen.
- The player moves a horizontal paddle left or right.
- When the ball hits the paddle it will bounce upward in an opposite angle
- when the ball hits a brick, the brick is destroied and the player is awarded 1 point, and the ball bounces off in an opposite angle.
- The player has 3 lives, when the ball passes below the bottom of the screen, they lose a life and the ball resets in the middle again.
- At 0 lives display "game over" and their final score
- If the ball hits a screen side it bounces off in an opposite angle
- At the top of the screen show the score and number of lives

Also note:

- game should be web-based (can use phaser as a library)
